Frequently Asked Questions
Who is PB for?
PB is for anyone who’s ever wondered why it’s so difficult to meet new people in their city.
What does it do?
PB is a place where users can find and create hangouts to make friends with people in their city.
How does it work?
People like you can create casual hangouts by giving us a simple description of what you want to do, including a time and place. Other people can browse the site for fun-looking hangouts, scope out who's going, and RSVP. It’s that easy.
Who can use the site?
Right now the service only available in a limited Beta to residents of Pittsburgh, PA.
Why do I need to connect with Facebook?
We're working hard to implement a feature that will filter hangouts by how similar you are with other people going. We’re going to use your Facebook “likes” to for our matching algorithm. This will make discovering which neighbors are like you even easier.
Why does your site suck so much?
This is a very early version of the site, that we've launched to get feedback. If you’ve found a bug or have a suggestion, please drop us a line.
Who are you anyway?
Visit our about page or any of our hangouts to get to know us better.
