What is PBandMe?
PBandMe is a place for users to find and create get-togethers in their city.
How does it work?
You can start your own hangout by giving us a simple description of what you want to do, including a time and place. Other people can browse the site for fun-looking hangouts, check who's going, and RSVP. It’s that easy.
Who can use the site?
Right now the service only available to people who live in Pittsburgh PA.
Why do I need to connect with Facebook?
We're working hard to implement a way of filtering hangouts by how similar you are with other people going. This will make discovering people you want to hang with even easier.
Why does your site suck so much?
This is a very early version of the site, that we've launched to get feedback. If you’ve found a bug or have a suggestion, please drop us a line.
